02 — our story

Moments I'll never forget

nights in

Chilling

Those nights we'd just lay in bed watching Avatar: The Last Airbender or whatever Korean drama, we was locked in, I didn't think of it as a big deal back then but looking back, those were some of my best moments. Just us, no fuss but comfort in each other's company.

hair days

The Tears (lol)

I'd cry every single time you did my hair and we both know it, don't lie. I'm sure it wasn't easy on either of us but somehow that's a memory I look back on and smile deep down. You showed up for the small things and that mattered.

one day

You Stepped In

I don't know if you even remember this but I do. Dad was upset, things escalated, and you came and put a stop to it while i was trying to run in a corner on the bed. My legs were already bruised up but you probably don't know how much that moment meant to me, or how long I've carried it. But I have and aways will.
